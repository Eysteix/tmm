# Taste Mummies Made Restaurant (TMM) Project

## Project Overview

**Taste Mummies Made Restaurant (TMM)** is a breakfast delivery service that brings homemade, nutritious meals directly to customers. The brand combines traditional cooking values with modern technology to provide healthy, affordable breakfast options.

## Brand Story

### Founder's Journey
The restaurant was founded by **Mr. Selasi Atakey Y. Christopher**, whose entrepreneurial journey began at age 12 when he helped his mother with her roadside koko (porridge) business in Nyive, a border town. This early experience instilled in him:
- Discipline and work ethics
- Respect for customers
- Understanding of honest business practices

His culinary passion was further developed through formal education in Home Economics during Junior High School, where he gained foundational knowledge in:
- Food preparation
- Hygiene standards  
- Nutrition principles

The official planning for TMM began on **February 25th, 2025** during a lecture session with support from friends, loved ones, and believers in the vision.

## Service Model

### Operating Schedule
- **Ordering Hours**: Daily 10:00 AM - 9:30 PM
- **Delivery Hours**: Daily 6:30 AM - 9:30 AM
- **Home-Cooking Service**: Fridays & Saturdays only (requires 2+ days advance notice)

### Target Customers
The menu is specifically designed for three distinct customer segments:

1. **Babies (6+ months)**
2. **Diabetes-Free Customers** 
3. **Diabetic Patients** (Special Care)

## Menu Categories

### Babies (6 Months & Above)
**Regular Menu:**
- Awusaa Koko (no sugar) with mashed bread, optional soft boiled egg
- Rice & Wheat Porridge (no sugar) with mashed bread
- Oat Porridge (no sugar) with mashed bread

**VIP Menu:**
- Awusaa Koko with milk and mashed bread
- Rice & Wheat Porridge with milk and mashed bread
- Oat Porridge with milk/groundnut and mashed bread
- Mini fruit pur√©e (banana or pawpaw)

### Diabetes-Free Customers
**Regular Menu:**
- Awusaa Koko with koose or bread (2-6 slices)
- Rice & Wheat Porridge with koose or bread
- Oat Porridge with koose or bread
- Toasted/plain bread with koose or fried egg
- Hot Lipton tea with sugar and milk

**VIP Menu:**
- Awusaa Koko with milk & groundnut, served with bread or koose
- Rice & Wheat Porridge with milk & groundnut, served with bread or koose
- Oat Porridge with milk & fruit topping
- Toasted bread with koose & egg
- Hot Lipton tea with milk & snack box

### Diabetic Patients (Special Care)
**Regular Menu:**
- Awusaa Koko (no sugar) with unsweetened groundnut
- Rice & Wheat Porridge (no sugar) with unsweetened groundnut
- Oat Porridge (no sugar) with unsweetened groundnut
- Wheat bread (2-4 slices) with boiled or low-oil egg
- Hot Lipton tea (no sugar), lemon optional

**VIP Menu:**
- Awusaa Koko with skimmed milk and unsweetened groundnut
- Rice & Wheat Porridge with skimmed milk and unsweetened groundnut
- Oat Porridge with skimmed milk & fruit topping
- Toasted wheat bread with boiled egg & vegetables
- Herbal tea (no sugar)
- Special Diabetic Care Box
